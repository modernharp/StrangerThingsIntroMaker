## ✨ Features

- **Iconic Aesthetics:** Transforms custom text into cinematic, 80s-inspired animated intros with dramatic transitions and the instantly recognizable glowing red typography.
- **In-Browser Rendering:** The animation is generated and played entirely on your own device—no video rendering servers required.
- **No Sign-Up Required:** 100% free to use. Just type your text and watch it glow.
- **Shareable Links:** Generates a unique URL for your custom intro so you can easily share your creation with friends.
